Welcome to Wallstreet DAO—where innovation meets community. As a premier university DAO, we harness the power of decentralized technology to drive collaboration and engagement within the academic realm. Dive into a dynamic ecosystem that features:

Blogs 📚: Explore insights and trends from the forefront of blockchain and decentralized finance.
Events 🏆: Join our exciting events and hackathons designed to inspire and connect.
Project Showcase: Discover cutting-edge projects and initiatives spearheaded by our vibrant community.
News 📰: Stay updated with the latest developments and announcements from the Wallstreet DAO.
Digiboard: Collaborate and interact with fellow members through our digital bulletin board.
Reviews: Share and read reviews on tools, technologies, and events that matter to you.
Stress Test: Participate in challenges that push the boundaries of innovation.
Mint NFT 🧸: Get exclusive access to mint and showcase unique NFTs, adding to your digital collection.
Join us on this transformative journey as we build the future of decentralized communities together. Welcome to Wallstreet DAO, where every idea counts!

Frontend: React, TypeScript
Backend: Node.js, Express
Blockchain Integration: Wagmi, Alchemy API, Polygon ZK-EVM
Deployment: Vercel / Netlify
